Overview

Purpose

This page is to help myself along with anyone else who would like to use this website take better control of their time by structuring their days. It will consist of some options of productive time tasks, as well as a to do list to benefit the users to keep track of the things they want to accomplish.

Audience

The audience will consist of those who wish to be more Mindful of their time and wish to accomplish more within a day or within weeks. This can consist of professionals as well as students, and anyone, striving to be more aware of where there time goes.

Dynamic elements

I am currently unsure of all the elements that will be required in order for me to create my project but at this moment I believe it will require: Objects, arrays, array methods, forEach and filter. How are use JavaScript logic to create new elements for to do items. I will also use JavaScript remove items that have been finished or mark items off.

Home page

Welcome to MyTime, This webpage is designed to help you keep track of your time, this to do list and calendar helps you keep track of what you want to or need to accomplish. At any point in time you can move to the calendar to add the information you want and begin your did you list. Rest of the concept on this page revolves around valuable concepts. I can benefit your daily routines.

Morning routines are valuable portions of your day where you can prepare yourself for the needs of the rest of the day. It is the time to take care of yourself and mentally prepare for your daily tasks.

Exercise, daily exercise is crucial for overall health and welfare. By scheduling portions of your day, and allow you to get a form of physical exercise in you are benefiting both physically and mentally. Exercise does not need to be intense, but rather movement every day is the most important part of exercising.

Daily scripture study is a valuable portion of your schedule, by finding time to spiritually uplift yourself every day you grow closer to a loving Heavenly Father and Savior, They will help you become uplifted and guide you throughout your day.
